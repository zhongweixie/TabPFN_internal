What the final evaluation changes

  • The inline eval_bank metric disabled ACT, so it measured a different inference path and did not predict final ACT AUC reliably.
  • λ=0.01 improves from fixed-k=4 AUC 0.7991 to ACT AUC 0.8157, winning on 31 of 38 datasets and trailing the external loop-k=4 reference by only 0.0016.
  • λ=0.05 improves from fixed-k=4 AUC 0.6661 to ACT AUC 0.8087, winning on 35 of 38 datasets; the model relies strongly on its ACT path.

Evaluation limitations

  • One official OpenML fold per dataset, not repeated folds.
  • Context capped at 1,000 rows, test set at 500 rows, features at 40.
  • The external loop-k=4 reference was evaluated under the same 38-task protocol and scored 0.8173 mean AUC.
  • Use these scores for internal checkpoint comparison, not a public TabArena leaderboard claim.
